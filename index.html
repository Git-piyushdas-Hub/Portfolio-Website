Intro

Hello, I'm Piyush Das, a fourth-year Computer Science Engineering student at VIT Bhopal College. During my college years, I had the opportunity to participate in a training program provided by Ethnus Codemithra through the college internship program. This intensive 2-month MERN stack certificate training program emphasized teaching fundamentals in the first month and applying those skills in a project during the second month. One of my notable projects from this program is "Subscriba Sphere," a newsletter signup website. I developed this website using Express and EJS as templates and integrated the Mailchimp API, showcasing my proficiency in web development. I chose backend development as my focus because it appeared straightforward to me, offering a clear path without too many complexities. I'm eager to continue exploring this field and applying my skills to real-world challenges. My passion for coding and technology, combined with my diverse experiences, motivates me to pursue a fulfilling career in the realm of Computer Science and Engineering.
